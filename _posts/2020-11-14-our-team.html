Hi! My name is Germán, and I was born in 1999 in Cantabria, northern Spain. From an early age I have been interested in video games, starting back in 2004 with Pokémon Emerald and, later on, with others such as Age of Empires and World of Warcraft, along my childhood friends. After finishing high school, I moved to Madrid to pursue my dreams of becoming a video game developer. A few months ago, I founded Serac Studio with some colleagues I met while studying at the URJC. There, I work as a Back-end Developer for all our projects. Since then, we have launched a fast-paced card game called Crystal Wars (which you can play on itch.io!) and are currently in the making of a fresh new 3D web-based game. You can contact me at germanSeracStudio@gmail.com for any business inquiry.
