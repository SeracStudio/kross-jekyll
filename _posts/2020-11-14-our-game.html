We are Serac Studio but our games won't freeze you out!

Now you are going to see the games made by our members and when and why they did it. Also you can play it!

"Crystal Wars"

In the heavenly world, Arké, the God of gods, is quiting his position and now their children, the elements' gods, are going to start a War to get it. In this cardgame you will choose which god is going to be the new God of gods. Fights with other players, defeat another gods and be the Choosen one. This game was developed by the whole team of Serac Studio as their first completed game. You can play it clicking here



"Untitled Board Game"

You embody Kanashi, a boy who has lost recently his girlfriend and he deals her death playing against his own mind to defeat the Grive. This game was developed by Pablo, Alejandro and Germán for the Spain Game Devs Jam II, the theme was the 'Grief'. You can play it clicking here



"Hell's Trial"

You just arrive to Hell and you're about to be tortured for all eternity, but a stroke of luck gives you a second chance. Being in the place of the Devil himself, you must judge other souls in grief, always in the fairest way possible. Depending on your actions, your last judgment will take one way or another. Justice is blind ... but not deaf. This game was developed by Pablo, Alejandro, Germán and Dilan for a minijam about Justice.You can play it clicking here



"Bicubic"

Explore the world of Bicubic, in which everything is cubic. Run everything you can and get all the worlds and characters. This game was developed by Dario. You can download it on the Play Story by clicking here
